Our Story & Vision

A Bridge to Antiquity

Founded under the umbrella of Shandong Mingzhi Education Consulting Co., Ltd., China Tea Culture Communication was born from a singular passion: to preserve and share the profound elegance of Chinese tea traditions with a global audience.

We are not merely merchants; we are curators of culture. Our team works directly with third-generation master potters in Yixing and porcelain artisans in Dehua to ensure every piece reflects the soul of its origin.

Direct from the Source

By eliminating middle-market distributors, we ensure that the value of every purchase goes directly to the artisans and farmers who dedicate their lives to this craft. Our logistics hub in China allows us to ship specialized, fragile ceramics to over 50 countries with unmatched care.

Our Global Mission

To transform the daily act of drinking tea into a cinematic ritual of mindfulness. Whether you are in the United States, Europe, or Southeast Asia, we bring the liquid jade of China's most prestigious mountains to your table.
