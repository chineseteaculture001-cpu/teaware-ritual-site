Our Story & Mission

Founded under the vision of Shandong Mingzhi Education Consulting Co., Ltd., China Tea Culture Communication is more than a purveyor of fine teaware. We are a bridge between the thousand-year-old wisdom of the East and the modern global lifestyle.

Based in the heart of China's ceramic heritage, we partner with master kilns in Yixing, Jingdezhen, and Dehua to bring authentic, high-saturation cinematic beauty to tea tables worldwide. From the United States to Vietnam, our mission is to make the "Art of the Pour" a universal language of peace and mindfulness.
