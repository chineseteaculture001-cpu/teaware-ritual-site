The Pillars of the Ritual

The Earth (Clay)

Yixing Zisha, the 'Purple Sand' clay, is porous and mineral-rich. Over years of use, the clay 'remembers' the tea, absorbing its oils and developing a unique patina that enhances every subsequent brew.

The Water (Flow)

Gongfu tea is the 'Art of Taking Time'. It's not just about drinking; it's about the precision of the pour, the temperature of the water, and the rhythm of the repeated steepings.

The Soul (Zen)

In the silence of the tea room, the chaotic world outside fades away. Tea culture in China is deeply intertwined with Zen philosophy—finding beauty in the imperfect and peace in the present moment.

Vibrant Heritage. Liquid Jade.

Our philosophy is grounded in the concept of 'Vibrant Heritage'. We believe that tradition shouldn't be a museum piece; it should be a living, breathing part of your day. 'Liquid Jade' represents the precious nature of the tea soup—a gem-like infusion that nourishes both body and spirit.

We specialize in 'Heritage Packaging'—providing not just a product, but a complete sensory experience. Every item arrives with a manual on history, craftsmanship, and the specific brewing ritual it was designed for.
